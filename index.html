Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 25, 2025

Simple Version

MetricMood does not collect, store, or share any of your data. Everything stays on your device.

What Data We Access

MetricMood accesses health data from Apple Health to display alongside your mood entries. This includes nutrition data (proteins, vitamins, minerals) and vital signs (heart rate, sleep, etc.).

What We Do With It

All data stays on your device. We don't send anything to servers, we don't collect anything, and we don't share anything with anyone.

Your Control

You can revoke access anytime in Settings → Privacy & Security → Health. Deleting the app removes all data.
